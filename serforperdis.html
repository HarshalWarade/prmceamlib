Services for person with disabilities

Materials Retrieval

Users with disabilities that prevent them from retrieving library materials may request at the Circulation Counter that the materials be retrieved for them.

Online Renewals/ Reservations and other services

Users with disabilities may request the Circulation Counter staff for help in checking OPAC, placing holds on books, online renewals, and other such services. They can directly issue books from stack area and not required to visit issuing counter. On every computer system Screen Reader software NVDA is available which will assist students with disabilities to read the study material including Story Books.
